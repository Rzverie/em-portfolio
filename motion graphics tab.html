University

An animation about my college applications. Made by animating an image of google search and adding 3D text to pair with the image. Then, adding 3D effects to websites of colleges I am applying to. I also made a custom overlay using RGB lines and by adding screen effects onto the drawn lines.

Dynamic Lighting

3D tower animation showing dynamic lighting. Made by creating a square and then adding 3D effects onto it so I could model the movement of the tower. I then added a "light" object (imagine a ball with properties of light) and I animated the movement of the light to create shadows and light around the tower.

Flame

Hand-drawn and animated candle flame. Made by drawing out the shapes of the candle in static form, and then by creating the flame as an object with a blend into the background and extra shading and gradient effects with grain onto an overlay to the candle.

Works In Progress

A compilation of all of my favorite music videos over the past decade. Made by mixing jungle and nature scenery with my favorite clips from music videos, along with effects added on top. The song "Electric Feel" is in the background, using the original studio mix to highlight the feel of the original version of the song, which isn't as widely appreciated by the public.
